# Bananabread

Persons: 4

## Ingredients

* 1 tbsp cinnamon
* 2 tbsp baking powder
* 2 eggs
* 3 bananas (brown preferred)
* 150 g flour (all purpose)
* 50 g sugar (white)
* 100 g olive oil (extra virgin preferred)
* Pinch of salt

Optional

* Walnuts (handful)

## Settings

* Oven 180 C (hot air)
* Baking time 45 min
